Introduction:

Bestbook is a modern and simple landing page template for showcasing and selling you e-book in easy way.

It is designed for e-book authors and marketers that love to show their product in a fantastic way. It can be used as whatever site related with launch of a prticular book. It had good sections that will help you to show the most important parts of your Book.

Features:

  • Made with bootstrap 5
  • Respinsive Template
  • Google Fonts are used
  • 6 Months support
